# Bildprompts für die Oberkapitel

Sieben einsatzfertige Prompts, je ein Header-Bild pro Oberkapitel. Stil, Palette und Negative-Prompt sind direkt im Prompt enthalten, damit alles in einem Rutsch eingefügt werden kann.

## 1. Wahrscheinlichkeitsrechnung

```
A calm editorial cover illustration that visually summarizes probability
theory. Compose three iconic probability elements within a single balanced
scene: a Venn diagram of two overlapping circles on the left, a small
probability tree with two branching levels in the center top, and a
classic six-sided die in the lower right. Behind these elements draw a
faint smooth bell curve and a low row of small histogram bars to suggest
discrete and continuous distributions. Connect all elements with thin
guide lines on a subtle coordinate grid in the background.

Style: clean editorial illustration, minimalist flat design with a hint
of technical drawing, thin precise linework, generous whitespace, calm
and sober mood, suitable as a university course header.

Color palette: off-white background (#F5F4EF), steel blue primary shapes
and lines (#2E5B88), one coral red accent (#E7553C) used only on the
Venn intersection and the die's top face, dark navy outlines (#1B2C44),
light gray helper grid (#D9D9D9).

Composition: 16:9, centered subject, generous margins, no text, no
numbers, no labels, no logos, no watermarks, no people, no faces.

Negative prompt: photorealism, heavy 3D rendering, neon colors, glow,
gradients, busy patterns, handwritten notes, cartoon characters,
mascots, decorative ornaments.
```
